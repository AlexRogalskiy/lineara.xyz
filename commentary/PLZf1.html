]TA • WI -TE-JA- MU • U-QE-TI • JA-SA-SA-RA- ME • TA-NU-NI-KI-NA • NI-NU- NI • [•]-I-[•]

Only the vertial stroke is preserved for the last syllabogram of JA-SA-SA-RA-; it could be ME or NE.
